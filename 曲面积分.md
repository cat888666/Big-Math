# 第一类曲面积分
计算$\displaystyle \iint\limits_{\sum}f(x,y,z)dS$
如果曲面$\sum:z=z(x,y),则dS=\sqrt{ 1+(z'_{x})^{2}+ (z'_{y})^{2} }dxdy$
 $\displaystyle\iint\limits_{\sum}f(x,y,z)dS=\iint\limits_{D_{xy}}f(x,y,z(x,y))\sqrt{ 1+(z'_{x})^{2}+(z'_{y})^{2} }dxdy$
 $D_{xy}为曲面在xoy的投影$
 
## 题目
$\displaystyle\iint\limits_{\sum } \frac{1}{z}dS,\sum:x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}=a^{2}被平面z=h(0<h<a)截得$
$$
\begin{align*}
z=\sqrt{ a^{2}-x^{2}-y^{2} },z'_{x}=\frac{-x}{\sqrt{ a^{2}-x^{2}-y^{2} }},z'_{y}=\frac{-y}{\sqrt{ a^{2}-x^{2}-y^{2} }}\\ \\
dS=\sqrt{ 1+(z'_{x})^{2}+(z'_{y})^{2} }dxdy= \frac{a}{\sqrt{ a^{2}-x^{2}-y^{2} }}dxdy\\ \\
D_{xy}:x^{2}+y^{2}\leq a^{2}-h^{2}\\ \\
原式=a\iint\limits_{D_{xy}} \frac{1}{a^{2}-x^{2}-y^{2}}dxdy=a\int_{0}^{2\pi}  \, d\theta \int_{0}^{\sqrt{ a^{2}-h^{2} }} \frac{1}{a^{2}-r^{2}} r\, dr\\ \\
=2\pi a\ln \frac{a}{h}
\end{align*}
$$
# 第二类曲面积分
对于$\displaystyle\iint\limits_{\sum}R(x,y,z)dxdy,\sum:z=z(x,y)$
$\displaystyle \iint\limits_{\sum}R(x,y,z)dxdy=\pm \iint\limits_{D_{xy}}R(x,y,z(x,y))dxdy(上侧为+，下侧为-)$


# 高斯公式解决第二类曲面积分

$\displaystyle{\subset\!\supset} \mathllap{\iint\limits_{\sum}}Pdydz+Qdzdx+Rdxdy=\pm \iiint\limits_{\Omega}\left( \frac{ \partial P }{ \partial x }+\frac{ \partial Q }{ \partial y } +\frac{ \partial R }{ \partial z }\right)dxdydz$
$\Omega$由**封闭曲面**$\sum$围成，$\sum$为外侧时为+
不封闭时要加面使其封闭
## 题目
$\displaystyle \iint\limits_{\sum}xdydz+ydzdx+zdxdy,\sum:x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}=a^{2}(z\geq 0)上侧$
$$
\begin{align*}
加上\textstyle\sum _{1}:x^{2}+y^{2}\leq a,z=0\qquad\qquad\qquad\\ \\取下侧而不是上侧，这样才能与\sum围成封闭曲面\\ \\
因为z=0，所以dz=0，zdxdy=0\qquad\qquad\\ \\
原式={\subset \supset \,} \mathllap{\iint\limits_{\sum+\sum_{1}}}xdydz+ydzdx+zdxdy-\iint\limits_{\sum_{1}}xdydz+ydzdx+zdxdy\\ \\={\subset \supset\, } \mathllap{\iint\limits_{\sum+\sum_{1}}}xdydz+ydzdx+zdxdy-0\qquad\qquad\\ \\=+\iiint\limits_{\Omega} 1+1+1\,dxdydz=3V_{半球}=2\pi a^{3}
\end{align*}
$$
