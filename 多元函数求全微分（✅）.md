$z=f(x,y)全微分\qquad \qquad dz=\displaystyle\frac{ \partial z }{ \partial x }dx+\frac{ \partial z }{ \partial y }dy$
$u=f(x,y,z)全微分\qquad du=\displaystyle\frac{ \partial u }{ \partial x }dx+\frac{ \partial u }{ \partial y }dy+\frac{ \partial u }{ \partial z }dz$
[[偏导数（✅）#求一阶偏导|求偏导]]后乘上对应的d，再相加
## 题目

$\qquad z=xy+ \dfrac{x}{y}$
$$
\begin{align*}
\frac{ \partial z }{ \partial x }=y+\frac{1}{y} \qquad\frac{ \partial z }{ \partial y }=x-\frac{x}{y^{2}} \\ \\
dz=\displaystyle\frac{ \partial z }{ \partial x }dx+\frac{ \partial z }{ \partial y }dy\qquad\\ \\
dz=\left( y+\frac{1}{y} \right)dx+\left( x-\frac{x}{y^{2}}  \right)dy
\end{align*}
$$
