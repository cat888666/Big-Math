$已知曲面F(x,y,z),点(x_{0},y_{0},z_{0}),$[[偏导数#求一阶偏导\|求偏导]]得到$F'_{x},F'_{y},F'_{z}$
于是：
$$
\begin{align*}
切平面:F'_{x}(x_{0},y_{0},z_{0})(x-x_{0})+F'_{y}(x_{0},y_{0},z_{0})(y-y_{0})+F'_{z}(x_{0},y_{0},z_{0})(z-z_{0})=0\\ \\
法线: \frac{x-x_{0}}{F'_{x}(x_{0},y_{0},z_{0})}=\frac{y-y_{0}}{F'_{y}(x_{0},y_{0},z_{0})}=\frac{z-z_{0}}{F'_{z}(x_{0},y_{0},z_{0})}\qquad\qquad
\end{align*}
$$
