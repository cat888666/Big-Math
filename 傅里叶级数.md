f(x)周期为2π，傅里叶级数为：
$\displaystyle f(x)=\frac{a_{0}}{2}+\sum_{n=1}^\infty (a_{n}\cos(nx)+b_{n}\sin(nx))$
$\displaystyle a_{n}=\frac{1}{\pi}\int_{-\pi}^{\pi}f(x)\cos(nx)  \, dx$
$\displaystyle b_{n}=\frac{1}{\pi}\int_{-\pi}^{\pi}f(x)\sin(nx)  \, dx$
# 题目
$当0\leq x\leq \pi 时,f(x)=1+x,当-\pi<x<0 时,f(x)=1-x$
$$
\begin{align*}
因为f(x)是偶函数，所以b_{n}=0\qquad\qquad\\ \\
a_{0}={\frac{1}{\pi}} \int_{-\pi}^{\pi} f(x) \, dx= \frac{2}{\pi} \int_{0}^{\pi}(1+x)  \, dx=\pi+2\\ \\
a_{n}=\frac{2}{\pi}\int_{0}^{\pi}(1+x)\cos (nx)  \, dx=\frac{2}{n\pi}\int_{0}^{\pi}  (1+x)\, d\sin(nx)\\ \\
=\frac{2}{n\pi}\left( (1+x)\sin(nx)|_{0}^\pi-\int_{0}^{\pi} \sin(nx) \, dx  \right)=\frac{2}{n\pi}\left( 0+\frac{1}{n}\cos(nx)|_{0}^\pi \right)\\ \\
=\frac{2}{n^{2}\pi}(\cos(n\pi)-1)=\frac{2}{n^{2}\pi}((-1)^{n}-1)\\ \\
f(x)=\frac{\pi+2}{2}+ \frac{2}{\pi}\sum_{n=1}^\infty\left(  \frac{(-1)^{n}-1}{n^{2}} \right)\cos nx,x\in[-\pi,\pi]
\end{align*}
$$
# 和函数
$$
\begin{align*}
f(x)的傅里叶级数的和函数为:\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\\ \\
S(x)=\begin{cases}f(x),x为f(x)的连续点\\ \\
\dfrac{f(x^+)+f(x^-)}{2},x为f(x)的间断点
\end{cases}\\ \\
\end{align*}
$$
## 题目
$f(x)周期为2\pi ,当-\pi<x\leq \pi时，f(x)=x^{3},求S\left( -\dfrac{5}{2}\pi \right)$
$$
\begin{align*}
当-3\pi<x\leq -\pi时，f(x)=(x+2\pi)^{3}\\ \\
-\frac{5}{2}\pi不是间断点，S(x)=f(x)\\ \\
S\left( -\frac{5}{2}\pi \right)=f\left( -\frac{5}{2}\pi \right)=\left(  -\frac{1}{2}\pi \right)^{3}=-\frac{\pi}{8}
\end{align*}
$$
