$\displaystyle\frac{ \partial z }{ \partial x }=\frac{ \partial z }{ \partial u }\frac{ \partial u }{ \partial x }+\frac{ \partial z }{ \partial v }\frac{ \partial v }{ \partial x }$
同理得
$\displaystyle\frac{ \partial z }{ \partial y }=\frac{ \partial z }{ \partial u }\frac{ \partial u }{ \partial y }+\frac{ \partial z }{ \partial v }\frac{ \partial v }{ \partial y }$
## 例题
 $\qquad \displaystyle z=f(xy,x+2y),求\frac{ \partial z }{ \partial x }$
$$ \begin{align*} 令u=xy，v=x+2y \qquad \qquad \qquad \quad\\ \\ 
\displaystyle\frac{ \partial z }{ \partial x }=\frac{ \partial z }{ \partial u }\frac{ \partial u }{ \partial x }+\frac{ \partial z }{ \partial v }\frac{ \partial v }{ \partial x } \qquad \qquad \qquad\\ \\ 
\displaystyle\frac{ \partial z }{ \partial u }=f'_{u} \qquad \frac{ \partial z }{ \partial v }=f'_{v} \qquad \frac{ \partial u }{ \partial x } =y \qquad \frac{ \partial v }{ \partial x } =1\\  \\
\displaystyle代入原式得\frac{ \partial z }{ \partial x }=f'_{u}y+f'_{v} \qquad \qquad \qquad
\end{align*} $$
# 求二阶偏导
$\qquad z=2xy+\sin(x+y^2),求\displaystyle f''_{x x}=\frac{ \partial^2 z }{ \partial x^2 },f''_{xy}=\frac{ \partial^2 z }{ \partial x  \partial y },f''_{yy}=\frac{ \partial^2 z }{ \partial y^2}$
$$
\begin{align*}
求一阶偏导得f'_{x}=2y+\cos(x+y^2)\qquad f'_{y}=2x+\cos(x+y^2)2y\\ \\
对f'_{x}再求x的偏导得f''_{x x}=-\sin(x+y^2)\qquad \qquad \quad\qquad\\ \\
对f'_{x}再求y的偏导得f''_{x y}=2-\sin(x+y^2)2y\qquad \qquad \qquad  \\ \\
对f'_{y}再求y的偏导得f''_{y y}=-\sin(x+y^2)4y^2+2\cos(x+y^2)\qquad
\end{align*}
$$

# 多元抽象复合函数求二阶偏导

$\displaystyle z=f(2x-y,xy),求f''_{xy}=\frac{ \partial^2 z }{ \partial x  \partial y }$
$$
\begin{align*}
令u=2x-y,v=xy\qquad\qquad\qquad\quad\\ \\
\displaystyle f'_{x}=\frac{ \partial z }{ \partial x }=\frac{ \partial z }{ \partial u }\frac{ \partial u }{ \partial x }+\frac{ \partial z }{ \partial v }\frac{ \partial v }{ \partial x } \qquad \qquad \quad\\ \\ 
\displaystyle\frac{ \partial z }{ \partial u }=f'_{u} \qquad \frac{ \partial z }{ \partial v }=f'_{v} \qquad \frac{ \partial u }{ \partial x } =2 \qquad \frac{ \partial v }{ \partial x } =y\\  \\
\displaystyle代入原式得f'_{x}=2f'_{u}+f'_{v}y \qquad \qquad \qquad\\ \\
设g(2x-y,xy)=f'_{u}\quad h(2x-y,xy)=f'_{v}\qquad\\ \\
f''_{xy}=[2f'_{u}]'_{y}+[f'_{v}y]'_{y}=2g'_{y}+f'_{v}+h'_{y}y\qquad\\ \\
\end{align*}
$$
接下来就是[[对抽象函数g,h求y的偏导（✅）]]，[[偏导数（✅）#多元抽象复合函数求一阶偏导|方法同上]]

结果为
$$
\begin{align*}
g'_{y}=-g'_{u}+xg'_{v}\qquad h'_{y}=-h'_{u}+xh'_{v}\qquad\qquad\\ \\
代回原式得f''_{xy}=2(-g'_{u}+xg'_{v})+f'_{v}+(-h'_{u}+xh'_{v})y\\ \\
将g变回f'_{u}\qquad h变回f'_{v}\qquad\qquad\qquad\\ \\
f''_{xy}=2(-f''_{uu}+xf''_{uv})+f'_{v}+(-f''_{vu}+xf''_{vv})y\qquad
\end{align*}
$$
