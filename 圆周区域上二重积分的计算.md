当D与圆有关，或被积函数含有$x^{2}+y^{2}$时，利用极坐标计算
$\displaystyle\iint\limits_{D}f(x,y)dxdy,令x=r\cos\theta,y=r\sin\theta$，于是原式变为$\displaystyle\iint\limits_{D}f(r\cos\theta,r\sin\theta)\,r\quad dr d\theta$
把$r,\theta$的范围解出来，接下来就是[[非圆周区域上二重积分的计算#^b363f0|计算]],**先对$r$积分**
注意$f(x,y)$换元后要乘$r$

# 题目

$求\displaystyle\iint\limits_{D}\sqrt{ x^{2}+y^{2} }dxdy,D: a^{2}\leq x^{2}+y^{2}\leq b^{2}$

$$
\begin{align*}
令x=r\cos\theta,y=r\sin\theta \quad\qquad\quad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\\ \\
原式=\iint\limits_{D}r^{2}dr d\theta \qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\quad\qquad\qquad\\ \\
a\leq r\leq b,0\leq\theta\leq 2\pi \qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\\ \\
\iint\limits_{D}r^{2}dr d\theta=\int_{0}^{2 \pi}  \, d\theta \int_{a}^{b}  \,r^{2} dr\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\\ \\  
\int_{a}^{b}  \,r^{2} dr=\left[ \frac{1}{3}r^3 \right]_{a}^b=\left( \frac{1}{3}b^3-\frac{1}{3}a^3 \right)\qquad\qquad\qquad\quad\\ \\
\int_{0}^{2 \pi} \left( \frac{1}{3}b^3-\frac{1}{3}a^3 \right) \, d\theta=\left( \frac{1}{3}b^3-\frac{1}{3}a^3 \right)\int_{0}^{2 \pi} 1 \, d\theta =\left( \frac{1}{3}b^3-\frac{1}{3}a^3 \right)2 \pi 
\end{align*}
$$

# 题目二

$求\displaystyle \iint\limits_{D}xydxdy,D:1\leq x^{2}+y^{2}\leq  2x,y\geq 0$
先算$r与\theta$的范围
$$
\begin{align*}
令x=r\cos\theta,y=r\sin\theta \qquad\qquad\\ \\
1\leq r^{2}\leq 2r\cos\theta,\quad r\sin\theta\geq 0\qquad\\ \\
由1\leq r^{2}\leq 2r\cos\theta得1\leq r\leq 2\cos\theta \quad\\ \\
因为r\geq  1,所以\cos\theta \geq \frac{r}{2}\geq \frac{1}{2}\qquad\\ \\
又因为r\sin\theta\geq 0，所以0\leq\theta\leq \frac{\pi}{3}\quad\\
\end{align*}
$$
接下来直接计算
$$
\begin{align*}
原式=\iint\limits_{D}r^{3}\cos\theta \sin\theta \quad dr d\theta
=\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{3}}\cos\theta \sin\theta  \, d\theta \int_{1}^{2\cos\theta} r^{3}\, dr\qquad\\ \\
 \int_{1}^{2\cos\theta} r^{3} \, dr=  = \left[ \frac{1}{4}r^4 \right]_{1}^{2\cos\theta}=\ \left( 4\cos^ 4\theta- \frac{1}{4} \right) \qquad\qquad\\ \\
 \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{3}}  \cos\theta \sin\theta  \left( 4\cos^ 4\theta- \frac{1}{4} \right)\, d\theta =4\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{3}}  \cos^5\theta\ \sin\theta \,d\theta- \frac{1}{4}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{3}}  \cos\theta \sin\theta \,d\theta\\ \\=-4\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{3}}  \cos^5\theta\  \,d\cos\theta+ \frac{1}{4}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{3}}  \cos\theta  \,d\cos\theta\\ \\
 =-\frac{2}{3}[\cos^6\theta]_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{3}}+ \frac{1}8{}[\cos^2\theta]_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{3}}= \frac{9}{16}\qquad\qquad
\end{align*}
$$
