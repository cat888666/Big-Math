已知非圆周区域D，求$\displaystyle\iint\limits_{D}f(x,y)dxdy$
1、分类区域D：做一条垂直于x轴的直线，与D的**边界最多只有两个交点**，则为$D_{x}$
同理，做一条垂直于y轴的直线，与D的**边界最多只有两个交点**，则为$D_{y}$
![[Screenshot_20260304_111247.jpg]]

$\displaystyle\iint\limits_{Dx}f(x,y)dxdy=\int_{a}^{b}  \, dx\int_{y_{1}(x)}^{y_{2}(x)}f(x,y)  \, dy$
$\displaystyle\iint\limits_{Dy}f(x,y)dxdy=\int_{c}^{d}  \, dy\int_{x_{1}(y)}^{x_{2}(y)}f(x,y)  \, dx$
先将后面的积出来，再代入前面的积分计算

# 题目

$求\displaystyle \iint\limits_{D}xy\,dxdy\qquad D由x=2,y=1,y=x围成$
$$
\begin{align*}
画出草图，判断D的类型，发现都可以，按D_{x}做\quad\qquad\qquad\\ \\
a=1,b=2,y_{1}(x)=1,y_{2}(x)=x\quad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\\ \\
\iint\limits_{D}xy\,dxdy=\int_{1}^{2}  \, dx\int_{1}^{x}  xy\, dy\quad\qquad\qquad\qquad\quad\qquad\\ \\
先算后面: \int_{1}^{x}  xy\, dy=x\int_{1}^{x} y \, dy=x\left[  \frac{1}{2} y^{2} \right]_{1}^x=x\left(  \frac{1}{2}x^{2}-\frac{1}{2}  \right)\qquad\\ \\
代入前面: \int_{1}^2{}  x\left(  \frac{1}{2}x^{2}-\frac{1}{2}  \right)\, dx =\frac{1}{2}\int_{1}^2 ( x^{3}-x ) \, dx =\frac{1}{2}\left[ \frac{1}{4}x^4-\frac{1}{2}x^{2} \right]_{1}^2\\ \\
结果为 \frac{9}{8}\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad
\end{align*}
$$

如果按$D_{y}$做
$$
\begin{align*}
c=1,d=2,x_{1}(y)=y,x_{2}(y)=2\qquad\qquad\\ \\
\iint\limits_{D}xy\,dxdy=\int_{1}^{2}  \, dy\int_{y}^{2}  xy\, dx\qquad\qquad\\ \\
\int_{y}^{2}  xy\, dx=y\int_{y}^2 x  \, dx =y\left[  \frac{1}{2} x^{2} \right]_{y}^2=y\left( 2- \frac{1}{2}y^{2} \right)\\ \\
\int_{1}^{2} y\left( 2- \frac{1}{2}y^{2} \right) \, dy=\left[ y^{2}-\frac{1}{8} y^4\right]_{1}^2 \qquad\\ \\
结果为 \frac{9}{8}\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\quad
\end{align*}
$$
# 更高难度的题目

某些积分如$\displaystyle\int \frac{\sin x}{x} \, dx ,\, \int \frac{\cos x}{x}dx,\, \int e^{ -x^{2} }dx,\,\int e^{ x^{2} }dx$
无法算出，遇到时需要改变D的类型

$\displaystyle求\iint\limits_{D} \frac{\sin y}{y}dxdy,D由x=y^{2},y=x围成$
$若按照D_{x}来做$
$$
\begin{align*}
a=0,b=1,y_{1}(x)=x,y_{2}(x)=\sqrt{ x }\\ \\
\iint\limits_{D} \frac{\sin y}{y}dxdy=\int_{0}^{1}  \, dx\int_{x}^{\sqrt{ x }} \frac{\sin y}{y}dy\\ \\
后面的无法计算，不能按照D_{x}来做
\end{align*}
$$
尝试$D_{y}$
$$
\begin{align*}
c=0,d=1,x_{1}(y)=y^{2},x_{2}(y)=y\qquad\qquad\quad\\ \\
\iint\limits_{D} \frac{\sin y}{y}dxdy=\int_{0}^{1}  \, dy\int_{y^{2}}^{y} \frac{\sin y}{y}dx\qquad\quad\qquad\\ \\
\int_{y^{2}}^{y} \frac{\sin y}{y}dx= \frac{\sin y}{y}\int_{y^{2}}^{y} 1 \, dx= \frac{\sin y}{y}[x]_{y^{2}}^y=\frac{\sin y}{y}(y-y^{2})\\ \\
\int_{0}^{1} \frac{\sin y}{y}(y-y^{2}) \, dy =\int_{0}^{1}\sin y(1-y)  \, dy\qquad\qquad\\ \\
结果为1-\sin 1\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad
\end{align*}
$$
