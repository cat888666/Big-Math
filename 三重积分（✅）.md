# 投影法（先一后二）
$$
\begin{align*}
\iiint\limits_{\Omega} \frac{1}{(1+x+y+z)^3}dxdydz\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\\ \\
其中\Omega由平面x=0，y=0,z=0,x+y+z=1围成\qquad\qquad\qquad\\ \\
1、先画\Omega草图\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\quad\\ \\
2、定D_{xy}穿z,\Omega==\begin{cases}(x,y)\in D_{xy}(D_{xy}为\Omega在xoy面的投影) \\ \\
0\leq z\leq1-x-y
\end{cases}\qquad\qquad\qquad\\ \\
3、先一后二: \iiint\limits_{\Omega} \frac{1}{(1+x+y+z)^3}dxdydz=\iint\limits_{D_{xy}}dxdy\int_{0}^{1-x-y} \frac{1}{(1+x+y+z)^3} \, dz \\ \\
\int_{0}^{1-x-y} \frac{1}{(1+x+y+z)^3} \, dz=- \frac{1}{2} \left( \frac{1}{1+x+y+z} \right)^2|_{0}^{1-x-y}=-\frac{1}{8}+\frac{1}{2(1+x+y)^{2}}\\ \\
\iint\limits_{D_{xy}}\left( -\frac{1}{8}+\frac{1}{2(1+x+y)^{2}} \right)dxdy,接下来是对二重积分的计算\\ \\
\int_{0}^{1}  \, dx\int_{0}^{1-x}  \, \left( -\frac{1}{8}+\frac{1}{2(1+x+y)^{2}} \right)dy=\int_{0}^{1} \left( \frac{x-1}{8} +\frac{1}{2+2x}-\frac{1}{4}\right) \, dx  \\ \\= \frac{1}{2}\left( \ln 2-\frac{5}{8} \right)\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad
\end{align*}
$$

# 平面截割法(先二后一)
$\displaystyle\iiint\limits_{\Omega}z^{2}dxdydz,\Omega:x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}\leq 1$
当被积函数为$z$的一元函数(只与z有关)时
1.画出草图
2.定$z穿D_{z}\,$,用平行与$xoy$的平面去割$\Omega$
3.先二后一计算
$$
\begin{align*}
用平行与xoy的平面去割\Omega \qquad\quad\quad\\ \\
\Omega:-1\leq z\leq 1,(x,y)\in D_{z}=x^{2}+y^{2}\leq 1-z^{2}\\ \\
\iiint\limits_{\Omega} z^{2}dxdydz=\int_{-1}^{1}  \, d\iint\limits_{D_{z}}z^{2}dxdy\qquad\\ \\
\iint\limits_{D_{z}}z^{2}dxdy=z^{2}\iint\limits_{D_{z}}1\,dxdy\qquad\qquad\\ \\
因为\iint\limits_{D}1\,dxdy=S_{D}(区域D的面积)\qquad\\ \\
所以z^{2}\iint\limits_{D_{z}}1\,dxdy=z^{2}(\pi(1-z^{2}))\qquad\\ \\
\int_{-1}^{1} z^{2}(\pi(1-z^{2})) \, dz=\frac{4}{15}\pi \qquad 
\end{align*}
$$

# 柱面坐标
$\displaystyle\iiint\limits_{\Omega}x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}dxdydz,\,\Omega为圆柱体:x^{2}+y^{2}\leq 1,-1\leq z\leq 1$
当积分区域为圆柱体,圆锥体,旋转体时,用柱面坐标
1.画出草图
2.换元$x=r\cos\theta,y=r\sin\theta,z=z,\theta 为与x轴的夹角$
此时$dxdydz=r \,\,dr d\theta dz$
3.三次积分公式计算
$$
\begin{align*}
令x=r\cos\theta,y=r\sin\theta,z=z\\ \\
\Omega:0\leq\theta\leq 2\pi,0\leq r\leq 1,-1\leq z\leq 1\\ \\
原式=\iiint\limits_{\Omega} (r^{2}+z^{2})r\,\,dr d\theta dz\\ \\
=\int_{0}^{2\pi}  \, d\theta \int_{0}^{1} r \, dr \int_{-1}^{1} (r^{2}+z^{2}) \, dz  \\ \\
=\frac{5}{3}\pi \qquad\qquad
\end{align*}
$$
# 球坐标
$\displaystyle\iiint\limits_{\Omega}xyz\,\,dxdydz,\Omega:x^{2}+y^{2}+z^{2}=1与三个坐标面围成的第一卦限区域$
1.画出草图
2.换元:$x=r\sin\varphi \cos\theta,y=r\sin\varphi \sin\theta,z=r\cos\varphi(\varphi为与z轴的夹角,\theta为与x轴的夹角)$
$dxdydz=r^{2}\sin\varphi dr d\varphi d\theta$
3.用三次积分公式:
$\displaystyle\iiint\limits_{\Omega}f(x,y,z)=\int_{\theta_{1}}^{\theta_{2}}  \, d\theta \int_{\varphi_{1}}^{\varphi_{2}}  \, d\varphi \int_{r_{1}}^{r_{2}} F(\theta,\varphi,r) r^{2}\sin\varphi\, dr$

$$
\begin{align*}
x=r\sin\varphi \cos\theta,y=r\sin\varphi \sin\theta,z=r\cos\varphi\\ \\
\Omega:0\leq\theta\leq \frac{\pi}{2},0\leq\varphi\leq \frac{\pi}{2},0\leq r\leq  1\\ \\
原式=\iiint\limits_{\Omega} r\sin\varphi \cos\theta r\sin\varphi \sin\theta r\cos\varphi r^{2}\sin\varphi\,\,dr d\varphi d\theta\\ \\ 
=\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \sin\theta \cos\theta \, d\theta \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}  \sin^3\varphi \cos\varphi\, d\varphi \int_{0}^{1} r^5 \, dr\\ \\
=\frac{1}{48}\qquad\qquad\qquad\qquad
\end{align*}
$$
